How It Works

UseMySound is a marketplace where creators can buy, sell and license music directly.

Artists upload their tracks, set prices and define how their music can be used. Buyers can explore sounds, preview them and choose the right license.

Music can be sold as exclusive, non-exclusive or rented for temporary use, depending on the needs of the project.

Secure payments ensure that creators get paid, while buyers receive instant access to high-quality music.

This creates a simple system where artists earn from their work and creators get unique sound for videos, games and content.
