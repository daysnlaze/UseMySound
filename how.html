How It Works

UseMySound is a marketplace where creators can buy, sell and license music directly.

Artists upload their tracks, set prices and choose how their music can be used. Buyers can explore sounds, preview them and purchase the right license.

Music can be sold as exclusive, non-exclusive or rented for temporary use. Each option gives different rights depending on the project.

Payments are handled securely, and the creator receives the money after delivery.

This allows musicians to earn from their work, while creators get instant access to unique music for videos, games and content.
